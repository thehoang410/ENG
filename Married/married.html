Marriage in Vietnam

On the wedding day, red is the main color chosen by many couples for weddings.

Rings for both men and women is to wear the wedding ring on the ring finger.
The wedding ceremony usually lasts for 1 day and 1 night at the bride's house, organized by the oldest uncle in the girl's family or the village elder.

The wedding day usually takes place in two places,
at the Trai's house called the Wedding Ceremony or the Marriage Ceremony,
and at the Girl's House called the Vu Quy Ceremony. Usually the number of dishes ranges from 5-7 dishes based on the requirements of the party owner such as: Starter: mixed vegetable salad, salad,... Main dish: steamed/fried shrimp, roasted pigeon, ball soup,. Dessert: fruit, sweet soup, ...




Marriage in Vietnam at ethnic Mang

The Mang prefer to hold weddings after the harvest season. They choose auspicious days between the 10th and 3rd lunar month. On the wedding day, the groom’s delegation brings offerings to the bride’s family: 20 silver coins, 200 kg of pork, chicken, some roasted squirrels and rats, a large container of wine, 20 kg of glutinous rice, and many pieces of cloth.

Rings for both men and women is to wear the wedding ring on the ring finger.
The wedding ceremony usually lasts for 1 day and 1 night at the bride's house, organized by the oldest uncle in the girl's family or the village elder.

When they arrive at the bride’s home, the groom and his representatives bow to the bride’s grandparents, parents, and relatives to thank them for raising the girl and ask permission to bring the bride to their home. Before the groom’s delegation and the bride leave her house, her relatives spray water and mud on them to hide them from the Jade Emperor. Otherwise, he will see who they are and prevent their wedding.

The final part of the wedding ceremony is a singing and dancing session to wish the couple happiness. In the past a Mang wedding was spread over 4 days at the houses of the bride and the groom. This has been shortened to 2 days to fir a more modern lifestyle.
